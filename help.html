The earth love vegans! (We do too!)

Nothing is more devastating to the earth than the mass production and consumption of meat and other animal products. If everyone changed their habits to include even one vegan day a week, the results would be huge!

Humans consume a lot. Food, clothes, stuff. It makes a lot of garbage. Stop it. Use what you have. And compost everything you can! We love it, and the earth loves it too!
